WeChat: alecwnicol · WhatsApp: (+852) 60642429 · awnicol@ust.hk

Alec Nicol is currently a PhD candidate in bioengineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Alec’s research experiences span a variety of fields including synthetic chemistry, materials characterization, semiconductor physics, optics, nanomedicine, biochemistry and genetics. Alec is an inventor on 2 patents and has published 7 peer-reviewed articles. Alec is a cofounder of Mamosound, a Hong Kong startup with a social mission to provide equal access to early breast cancer detection through the implementation of new cost saving technologies. Alec has interned at The National University of Singapore, Tsinghua University (China), Chiba University (Japan) and Rice University (Houston). Alec completed a triple major in chemistry, biochemistry and physiology at the University of Minnesota-Twin Cities.
