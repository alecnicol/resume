Dr. Alec Nicol

WeChat: alecwnicol · WhatsApp: (+852) 60642429 · awnicol@ust.hk

I am currently a PhD candidate in bioengineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. My research experiences have spanned a variety of fields including synthetic chemistry, materials characterization, semiconductor physics, optics, nanomedicine, biochemistry and genetics. I am an inventor on 2 patents and have published 7 peer-reviewed articles. I am the CEO and cofounder of Mamosound, a Hong Kong startup with a social mission to provide equal access to early breast cancer detection through the implementation of new cost saving technologies. I have previously held research positions at The National University of Singapore, Tsinghua University (China), Chiba University (Japan) and Rice University (Houston). I have completed a triple major in chemistry, biochemistry and physiology at the University of Minnesota-Twin Cities (USA). I’m passionate about translational research and improving the way innovations are spread. Toward this goal, I am currently pursuing a career as a patent attorney. More randomly, I have been a High School science bowl coach, spent over a combined year hammock camping am love spearfishing.
